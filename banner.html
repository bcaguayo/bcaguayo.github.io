KNOW YOUR FLAGS: A QUEER BANNER COMPENDIUM

PRIDE FLAG

THE RAINBOW FLAG, COMMONLY KNOWN AS THE PRIDE FLAG, IS A SYMBOL OF LESBIAN, GAY, BISEXUAL, TRANSGENDER, AND QUEER (LGBTQ+) PRIDE AND SOCIAL MOVEMENTS. IT CONSISTS OF SIX STRIPES: RED, ORANGE, YELLOW, GREEN, BLUE, AND VIOLET.

  • YEAR INTRODUCED: 1978
  • COLORS: RED, ORANGE, YELLOW, GREEN, BLUE, VIOLET
  • MEANING: DIVERSITY, INCLUSION, PRIDE

PROGRESS FLAG

THE PROGRESS PRIDE FLAG IS A VARIATION OF THE RAINBOW FLAG THAT INCORPORATES A CHEVRON ALONG THE HOIST THAT FEATURES BLACK, BROWN, LIGHT BLUE, PINK, AND WHITE STRIPES TO BRING FOCUS TO INCLUSION AND PROGRESS WITHIN THE LGBTQ+ COMMUNITY.

  • YEAR INTRODUCED: 2018
  • COLORS: RED, ORANGE, YELLOW, GREEN, BLUE, VIOLET, BLACK, BROWN, LIGHT BLUE, PINK, WHITE
  • MEANING: PROGRESS, INCLUSIVITY, DIVERSITY

BISEXUAL FLAG

THE BISEXUAL PRIDE FLAG IS A SYMBOL OF THE BISEXUAL COMMUNITY. IT CONSISTS OF THREE HORIZONTAL STRIPES: MAGENTA (TOP), LAVENDER (MIDDLE), AND ROYAL BLUE (BOTTOM).

  • YEAR INTRODUCED: 1998
  • COLORS: MAGENTA, LAVENDER, ROYAL BLUE
  • MEANING: ATTRACTION TO BOTH SAME AND OTHER GENDERS

GAY MAN FLAG

THE GAY MEN PRIDE FLAG IS A SYMBOL OF THE GAY MALE COMMUNITY. IT CONSISTS OF SEVEN HORIZONTAL STRIPES: DARK BLUE (TOP), LIGHT BLUE, GREEN, WHITE, YELLOW, ORANGE, AND RED (BOTTOM).

  • YEAR INTRODUCED: 2019
  • COLORS: DARK BLUE, LIGHT BLUE, GREEN, WHITE, YELLOW, ORANGE, RED
  • MEANING: DIVERSITY, INCLUSION, PRIDE OF GAY MEN

LESBIAN FLAG

THE LESBIAN PRIDE FLAG IS A SYMBOL OF THE LESBIAN COMMUNITY. IT CONSISTS OF SEVEN HORIZONTAL STRIPES: DARK ORANGE (TOP), ORANGE, LIGHT ORANGE, WHITE, LIGHT PINK, PINK, AND DARK PINK (BOTTOM).

  • YEAR INTRODUCED: 2018
  • COLORS: DARK ORANGE, ORANGE, LIGHT ORANGE, WHITE, LIGHT PINK, PINK, DARK PINK
  • MEANING: DIVERSITY, INCLUSION, PRIDE OF LESBIANS

PANSEXUAL FLAG

THE PANSEXUAL PRIDE FLAG IS A SYMBOL OF THE PANSEXUAL COMMUNITY. IT CONSISTS OF THREE HORIZONTAL STRIPES: PINK (TOP), YELLOW, AND BLUE (BOTTOM).

  • YEAR INTRODUCED: 2010
  • COLORS: PINK, YELLOW, BLUE
  • MEANING: ATTRACTION TO ALL GENDERS

TRANSGENDER FLAG

THE TRANSGENDER PRIDE FLAG IS A SYMBOL OF THE TRANSGENDER COMMUNITY. IT CONSISTS OF FIVE HORIZONTAL STRIPES: LIGHT BLUE (TOP), LIGHT PINK, WHITE, LIGHT PINK, AND LIGHT BLUE (BOTTOM).

  • YEAR INTRODUCED: 1999
  • COLORS: LIGHT BLUE, LIGHT PINK, WHITE, LIGHT PINK, LIGHT BLUE
  • MEANING: TRANSGENDER PRIDE AND IDENTITY

ASEXUAL FLAG

THE ASEXUAL PRIDE FLAG IS A SYMBOL OF THE ASEXUAL COMMUNITY. IT CONSISTS OF FOUR HORIZONTAL STRIPES: BLACK (TOP), GRAY, WHITE, AND PURPLE (BOTTOM).

  • YEAR INTRODUCED: 2010
  • COLORS: BLACK, GRAY, WHITE, PURPLE
  • MEANING: ASEXUALITY, COMMUNITY, PRIDE

NON-BINARY FLAG

THE NON-BINARY PRIDE FLAG IS A SYMBOL OF THE NON-BINARY COMMUNITY. IT CONSISTS OF FOUR HORIZONTAL STRIPES: YELLOW (TOP), WHITE, PURPLE, AND BLACK (BOTTOM).

  • YEAR INTRODUCED: 2014
  • COLORS: YELLOW, WHITE, PURPLE, BLACK
  • MEANING: NON-BINARY GENDER IDENTITY, COMMUNITY, PRIDE
