Famous Video Game Glitches

  • MissingNo. - Pokemon Red/Blue

    A glitched Pokemon that appears when the player performs a specific sequence of actions.

  • Big Head Mode - GoldenEye 007

    A cheat code that causes all characters' heads to become larger.

  • The Swingset Glitch - Grand Theft Auto IV

    A glitch that causes the player's vehicle to launch into the air when driven into a specific swingset.

  • The Missing Bridge - The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time

    A glitch that causes a bridge to disappear, making it impossible to progress in the game.
